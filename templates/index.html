Thank you for considering to participate in this study. The aim of this study is to investigate cognitive ability of aesthetic judgment under conditions of distraction. In this study, you will be presented with a number of images. After which you will be asked to complete some point-and-click tasks. The whole experiment should take no more than 5 minutes.
This is a class project being conducted by psychology students at the University of Melbourne. The results will remain anonymous and will not be published.
If you have any further enquiries, please contact Dr Philip Smith at philipls@unimelb.edu.au
